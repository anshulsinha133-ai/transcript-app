Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to VoxNote ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our VoxNote mobile application ("App").

By using VoxNote, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Audio Recordings: Voice recordings you create within the app for transcription purposes.
  • Account Information: Email address and authentication details when you create an account.
  • Transcripts: Text transcriptions generated from your audio recordings.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality and bug fixing.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app to improve our services.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide AI-powered transcription services for your recordings
  • Detect speakers and identify different voices in recordings
  • Translate Hindi and Marathi speech to English
  • Generate AI summaries of your transcripts
  • Store and sync your transcripts across sessions
  • Improve our transcription accuracy and app performance
  • Send important updates about the app

4. Third-Party Services

VoxNote uses the following third-party services to provide its features:

  • AssemblyAI: For speech-to-text transcription and speaker detection. Audio is temporarily processed on their servers and deleted after transcription.
  • OpenAI: For AI-powered translation and summary generation. Text data is processed according to OpenAI's privacy policy.
  • Supabase: For secure user authentication and transcript storage.

Each of these services has their own Privacy Policy governing the use of your data.

5. Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • All data is transmitted using encrypted HTTPS connections
  • Audio recordings are processed and immediately deleted after transcription
  • Transcripts are stored securely in encrypted databases
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only

🔒 Your audio recordings are never permanently stored on our servers. They are processed for transcription and immediately deleted.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as follows:

  • Audio Recordings: Deleted immediately after transcription (within minutes)
  • Transcripts: Stored until you delete them or close your account
  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Usage Data: Retained for up to 12 months for analytics

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Delete your transcripts or entire account at any time
  • Correction: Update or correct your personal information
  • Portability: Export your transcripts in a readable format
  • Opt-out: Opt out of non-essential data collection

8. Children's Privacy

VoxNote is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

We recommend reviewing this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
